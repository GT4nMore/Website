Maps are the space or arena where players confront one another. This term is used almost exclusively when describing multiplayer games. Maps in the Counter-Strike series pit Terrorists against Counter-Terrorists in real-world style locations.

In some map scenarios, players are awarded money for completing the map's objectives or by eliminating players from the opposing team.

Arsenal scenario maps rewards players with new weapons for completing objectives.
